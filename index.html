ABOUT PRAYUKTI

Prayukti is the annual technical fest of Graphic Era Hill University, Bhimtal — a celebration of curiosity, code, and innovation set against the hills of Uttarakhand.

The aim of this tech fest is to provide a platform for students to showcase their technical soundness, invention and innovation across their respective subjects and fields of interest.

Organizing a Student Technical Fest is a strategic initiative aimed at fostering innovation, creativity, and technical excellence among students. Such events provide a dynamic platform for knowledge sharing, skill development, and networking — essential components in today's competitive academic and professional landscapes.

Prayukti 2026 promises to be a grand and dynamic event, bringing together renowned professors, industry experts, and talented students from reputed colleges. The fest will feature a diverse range of competitions, workshops, and interactive sessions — an exceptional platform for learning, innovation, and collaboration. It will also give the students of our university valuable exposure and the opportunity to enhance their skills and broaden their perspective.
